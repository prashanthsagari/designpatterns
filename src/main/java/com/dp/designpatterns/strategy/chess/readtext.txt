OO Basics :
**********
Abstraction
Encapsulation
Polymorphism
Inheritance

OO Principles:
**************
Design Principle 1 : Identify the aspects of your application that vary and seperate them from what stays the same.
Design Principle 2 : Program to an interface, not an implementation.
Design Principle 3 : Favor composition over inheritance.

OO Pattern: Strategy
**********
Defines a family of algorithms, encapsulates each one, and makes them interchangeable. Strategy lets the algorithm vary independently from clients that use it.
